Top Places to Visit in Bali

Uluwatu Beach

Famous for its cliffs and scenic views, Uluwatu Beach is perfect for relaxation and water activities.

Uluwatu Temple

A beautiful Hindu temple perched on a cliff, offering breathtaking ocean views and traditional Kecak dance performances.

Tegallalang Rice Terraces

Explore the stunning rice terraces of Tegallalang, a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its lush greenery.
